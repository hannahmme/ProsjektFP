About us

COmeals is the future within sharing lunch among students. Our main goal is to create a platform where we encourage students to plan healthy and affordable lunches with fellow students.

Our vision is first and foremost to help students save money, but also to eat healthier and to become more social at school. We hope that COmeals will show students how to plan their meals to save money and throw away less food, as well as bringing people together to lessen the loneliness some students experience.
