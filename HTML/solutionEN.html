Solution

Scroll down

Our solution is COmeals - the app where you can collaborate on making lunch with other students! Make groups with friends, acquaintances or unknown students, distribute the weekdays and make lunch for all of the group members for the day assigned to you.


COmeals makes the collaboration with other students on lunch easy - it distributes the desired number of weekdays between the group members, keeps track of who’s making lunch for each day and when and where the group members are going to meet up. The app gives the group members the possibility to talk with each other in the chat-function. In addition, the app makes a financial settlement after every group member have submitted their expenses, and give information about how much money the group members owes or should have back.


How does COmeals solve the problem?

By collaborating on home cooked lunch meals with other students, you will use significantly less money than you do if you buy lunch from the cafeteria every day*. You as a student can now set aside more money for savings or use your money on exactly what you want. The need for a part-time job may then diminish. In addition, you save precious time by making lunch only one day a week!**


COmeals does not only contribute to save money and time, it also contributes to reduce food waste! By making lunch for more people than yourself, you will use more of the groceries that you have already bought in, than if you are making lunch for one person. Leftovers can now be eaten by fellow students instead of suffering of decay and then be thrown away. By throwing away less food you will also save more money.


In addition to saving time, money and the environment when making lunch with other students, COmeals can also help students who wish to become more social to find new arenas to be with other people. In the app the user will have the opportunity to make new groups with people they don’t know from before, thus forming new acquaintances.

** Applies if the number of group members is the same as the number of days you are going to be at school.
