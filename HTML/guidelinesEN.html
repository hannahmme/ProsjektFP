Follow these guidelines to have the best user experience with COmeals

Guidelines for use of COmeals

  • Every group can have a minimum of two and maximum of five group members.
  • The group has to agree on a meetup point and time, to avoid misunderstandings and unnecessary use of time.
  • Do you not have the opportunity to prepare lunch for your assigned day? You can then ask the other group members to switch day.
  • Each serving has to be between 300 g and 600 g.

Mark system:

  • If you don’t meet up to your day or forget to make the lunch without notifying the other group members in advance, you will get a mark. The mark is given in the app by one of the other group members.
  • If you get two marks in one month you will be suspended from the app for one month.
  • To avoid getting a mark you have to notify every member of the group within 4 PM the day before your assigned day. If you wake up sick on your assigned day, you still get a mark.
  • In case of sickness, if you have already bought in the food and made lunch, you still have to pay for the food yourself and you’ll get a mark (freeze the food if possible and serve it some other time).
