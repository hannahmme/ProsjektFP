About us

COmeals is the future within cooperating about lunch among students. Our vision is to create a platform where every student can feel welcome, where they can plan healthy and affordable lunches with fellow students.

Our goal is to make students eat more healthy and be more social at school. We hope this will lessen the loneliness some students experience at school, as well as helping them save money and most importantly, eat their lunch.
