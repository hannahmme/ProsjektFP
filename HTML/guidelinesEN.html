Follow these guidelines to have the best user experience with COmeals

Guidelines for using COmeals

  • Every group can have a minimum of two and a maximum of five group members.
  • The group has to agree on a meetup point and time, to avoid misunderstandings and unnecessary use of time.
  • If you do not have the opportunity to prepare lunch for your assigned day, you can switch days by asking other group members.
  • Each serving has to be between 300 and 600 grams.

Mark system:

  • If you do not meet up to lunch on the day you were supposed to make lunch, or forget to make lunch without notifying the other group members in advance, you will get a mark. The mark is given in the app by one of the other group members.
  • If you get two marks in one month you will be suspended from COmeals for one month.
  • To avoid getting a mark you have to notify every member of the group within 4 PM the day before your assigned day. If you wake up sick on your assigned day, you still get a mark.
  • In case of sickness, if you have already bought in the food and made lunch, you still have to pay for the food yourself and youâ€™ll get a mark (freeze the food if possible and serve it some other time).
